About Chess

Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on an 8x8 grid called a chessboard. The objective is to checkmate your opponent's king.

Best Chess Openings for Beginners

  • 1. Italian Game: 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bc4
  • 2. Ruy López: 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bb5
  • 3. Sicilian Defense: 1.e4 c5
  • 4. French Defense: 1.e4 e6
  • 5. Queen's Gambit: 1.d4 d5 2.c4
